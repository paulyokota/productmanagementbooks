Intercom on Product Management

Des Traynor and John Collins
Intercom, known for its great blog on product management has put together an ebook covering such topics as how to prioritize which features to build (and which ones to say no to), as well as how to set your new features up for success.
Get it from Intercom

The book is structured into four sections: Evaluate your product, When to say no to new features, Which new features to build, and Getting that feature used. Industry luminaries like Hunter Walk, Dharmesh Shah and Ryan Singer have praised it for its value for anyone who is either working in or interested in a product role. Best of all, they're giving it away for free.
